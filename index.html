S-CEM: Strategic Customer Experience Management

Strategic Customer Experience Management (S-CEM) is the natural evolution of the CEM methodology developed a decade ago to complement process centric CRM. S-CEM overcomes the weaknesses of CEM by rooting its initiatives in the corporate strategy, maintaining a constant focus on value generation and using a more holistic approach to improve the functional and emotional customer experience journey.

S-CEM integrates proven operational CEM tools into a strategic framework that helps management to take better decisions, manage better the ROI of CEM, bridge corporate silos and, most importantly, optimize the service value chain.
